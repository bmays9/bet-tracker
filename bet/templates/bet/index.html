Welcome to Bet Tracker

A site to track all of your football bets, while sharing and comparing your profitability with our community.

How The Site Works

As a registered user, you can add your football bets into the site so they are shared with other users.

When the match (or matches) have been played, simply update your bet with the result and and your balance will be adjusted accordingly. This site is only recoring and tracking the amounts you are betting, and does deal with any real money.

Guide To Tracking Your Bets

As a registered user, you can add a bet to the site with up to 6 matches (lines) in a single bet. The teams, your prediction and the odds of each line are entered individually.

Football Betting Guide

Track your bets on football games with ease! Here's how it works:

Bet Types
  • Home Win: Bet on the home team to win.
  • Away Win: Bet on the away team to win.
  • Draw: Bet on the match ending in a draw.
Understanding Odds

Odds are displayed in decimal format (e.g., 1.50, 2.25, 3.75).

Decimal odds show how much you’ll receive for every unit staked, including your original stake.

Example: If odds are 2.50 and you bet £10, your return will be £25.

How Returns Are Calculated

Your total return is calculated as:

Return = Stake × Odds

To find your profit:

Profit = Return - Stake
Example Bets
  • Bet on Home Win:
    • Odds: 1.80
    • Stake: £10
    • Return: £10 × 1.80 = £18
    • Profit: £18 - £10 = £8
  • Bet on Draw:
    • Odds: 3.25
    • Stake: £10
    • Return: £10 × 3.25 = £32.50
    • Profit: £32.50 - $10 = £22.50
  • Bet on Away Win:
    • Odds: 2.10
    • Stake: $10
    • Return: £10 × 2.10 = £21
    • Profit: £21 - $10 = £11
Odds will always be greater than 1.00. Track all your bets and enjoy responsibly!
