Welcome to Bet Tracker

A site to track all of your football bets, while sharing and comparing your profitability with our community.

How the Site Works

As a registered user, you can share your football bets with others by adding them to the site. Once the match (or matches) have been played, simply update your bet with the results, and your fictional balance will be adjusted accordingly.

Bet Tracker keeps a detailed record of all your bets, allowing you to track your performance and see how successful (or not) your bets have been. Please note that Bet Tracker does not involve real money— all balances and amounts displayed are purely for informational purposes.

Guide to Tracking Your Bets

As a registered user, you can create bets with up to six matches (or lines) in a single entry. Here's how to get started:

  1. Add Your Bet: Enter the stake amount for the bet. For each match, add the two teams, your prediction, and the corresponding odds.
  2. Update Your Bet: Navigate to the "View Bets" page and click the update button next to your bet. Update the status of each match line, as well as the overall bet status. If your bet is a winner, enter the "Settled Amount" to reflect your winnings.

With Bet Tracker, managing and reviewing your bets has never been easier!

Football Betting Guide

Track your bets on football games with ease! Here's how it works:

Bet Types
  • Home Win: Bet on the home team to win.
  • Away Win: Bet on the away team to win.
  • Draw: Bet on the match ending in a draw.
Understanding Odds

Odds are displayed in decimal format (e.g., 1.50, 2.25, 3.75).

Decimal odds show how much you’ll receive for every unit staked, including your original stake.

Example: If odds are 2.50 and you bet £10, your return will be £25.

How Returns Are Calculated

Your total return is calculated as:

Return = Stake × Odds

To find your profit:

Profit = Return - Stake
Example Bets
  • Bet on Home Win:
    • Odds: 1.80
    • Stake: £10
    • Return: £10 × 1.80 = £18
    • Profit: £18 - £10 = £8
  • Bet on Draw:
    • Odds: 3.25
    • Stake: £10
    • Return: £10 × 3.25 = £32.50
    • Profit: £32.50 - $10 = £22.50
  • Bet on Away Win:
    • Odds: 2.10
    • Stake: $10
    • Return: £10 × 2.10 = £21
    • Profit: £21 - $10 = £11
Odds will always be greater than 1.00. Track all your bets and enjoy responsibly!
